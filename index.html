Happy Mother's Day 💐

Celebrating the most amazing woman in my life

Hi Ma, Happy Mother’s Day! 💐 Alam mo, minsan nahihirapan akong i-express lahat ng nararamdaman ko, pero today gusto ko talagang sabihin nang buo: thank you for everything. Thank you sa lahat ng sakripisyo mo—yung mga oras na puyat ka para alagaan ako, yung mga times na inuuna mo needs ko kaysa sa sarili mo, at yung unconditional love na kahit anong mangyari, hindi nauubos. You’ve been my guide, my teacher, my best friend, and my safe place. Hindi ko man masabi araw-araw, pero sobrang grateful ako sa lahat ng ginagawa mo. You taught me how to be strong, how to be kind, and how to keep going kahit mahirap. Ikaw yung reason kung bakit may courage ako to chase my dreams. Ma, I want you to know na kahit tumanda ako, kahit saan ako mapunta, you’ll always be my home. I promise to make you proud and to give back kahit konti lang compared sa lahat ng binigay mo. Today is your day, and I hope you feel how loved and appreciated you are. You are the strongest, most beautiful, and most inspiring woman I know. I love you so much, Ma. ❤️

Made with love for Mom 🌸
